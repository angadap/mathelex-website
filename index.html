Safety

Safety first, always

Mathelex is built as a closed, distraction-free space for children — no ads, no external links, no data trade.

No ads, ever Nothing sold or shown to children — no banners, no video ads, no in-app popups.
No personal data No email or phone number captured from students — nothing to sell or share.
